**Webpack** is a **module bundler** that is used to compile JavaScript modules. It is capable of bundling, transforming, or packaging any resource or asset ¹. Once installed, you can interact with webpack either from its CLI or API ². 

Webpack is widely used in the industry and has a large community of contributors and users. It supports loaders to preprocess files such as json, jsx, es7, css, less, and more ³. 

If you are new to webpack, I recommend reading through the core concepts and guides section on their website ². You can also check out their comparison page to learn why webpack is a popular choice for module bundling ².
